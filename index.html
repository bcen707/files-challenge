The Metallic Genius: Iron Man

Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, is a billionaire as well as a brilliant engineer. He took after his father, Howard Stark, who was the CEO and owner of the corporation Stark Industries, which specialized in military weapons and other technical gadgets. One day after giving a weapons demo, Tony was captured by terrorists who forced him to build a weapon of mass destruction. In order to escape, Stark used the materials given to him to build a strong suit of armor. After escaping, Stark realizes the world needs protecting so he optimizes his suit and Iron Man was born.
